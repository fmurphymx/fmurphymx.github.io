Welcome to my webpage! I am an Associate Professor at the Faculty of Science, UNAM, specializing in Noncommutative Algebra, Group Theory, and Order Theory. I obtained my Doctorate in Mathematics from the Instituto de Matemáticas, UNAM, under the guidance of Dr. José Ríos and Dr. Francisco Raggi.

I have directed 13 theses at both Bachelor's and Master's levels, guiding students in diverse areas such as finance, machine learning, game theory, category theory, ring theory, order theory, and algebraic topology. I am passionate about both teaching and research, striving to foster a stimulating academic environment for my students while contributing to the advancement of knowledge in my field. .
